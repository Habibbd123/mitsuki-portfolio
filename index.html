01 / ABOUT

Narrative meets
infrastructure.

I'm Mitsuki — a Web3 growth architect who's spent the last few years living at the front lines of emerging protocols, helping them find their voice before the market does. My work spans ambassador programs, AI-driven identity systems, and the day-to-day craft of keeping a community alive: moderating, designing, writing, and shipping.

I care about the difference between noise and signal. Anyone can post a graphic; fewer people can build a campaign that compounds trust over months. That's the work I do — pairing sharp visual design with growth mechanics that are honest about what a protocol actually does.

Off the clock, I'm usually deep in a new AI tool, sketching motion concepts, or arguing (kindly) with a Discord mod queue at 2AM.
